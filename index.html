My creative journey began in Paris, where I studied general art and Graphic Design. I had the opportunity to work with talented people all over the world. At first I worked as a freelancer for agencies and international clients (Lula MAG, Yves Saint Laurent, Sofar Festival, Ekimedia, Cuisin et Vin de France, MPA MAG, Carribean Line and more). In 2019, I came back to my hometown Warsaw, where I worked for the Danish company Demant. Besides that, I worked with clients of my choice (Antoina Poppy, unpacking design, SEZONmag, PopTown, La Boutique de l'entrepreneur and others). In 2019 I started to develop frontend developpent as a complement to my professional skills. Since 2021, I have been in Sweden looking for new inspirations and challenges.
